All I am  
All I’ve been reduced to  
is a constant state of fear  
and anxiety and  
I can’t handle the way things are  
in my world  
in this place  
I’m all alone now  
in my nightmares  
because all you are  
is uncaring  
unfeeling  
and all I am  
is fear.
